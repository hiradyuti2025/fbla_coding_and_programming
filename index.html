How to Use Hometown Hub
Your guide to discovering local businesses
1
Explore the Map
Click the Map tab to see all businesses on an interactive map. Click any pin for a summary and deals. Use category chips to filter.
2
Browse the Directory
Click Directory to see all businesses as cards. Search by name or filter by category in the sidebar. Sort by rating, reviews, or active deals.
3
Save Your Favorites
Click the heart icon on any card to save it. View all saved businesses under Saved.
4
View Business Details
Click any card or map pin to see the full detail page — info, active deals, reviews, and star ratings.
5
Support Local & Minority-Owned
Filter by Black-Owned or Minority-Owned categories to specifically discover and support these businesses.
6
Chat with the Helper
Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right for quick answers about categories, deals, hours, and featured local businesses.
